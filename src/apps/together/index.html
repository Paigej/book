Name:

Let's DJ

Activity:

People in the same location can pick the songs to play at a party. There will be a queue of songs that people can upvote or downvote.

Number of Consumers:

As few as 5 or as many as 200

Skills:

Basic technological navigations skills and a good taste in music

Practicality:

A fun but practical app for all music lovers

Visibility:

There will be a queue of songs

Order:

The last picked songs will go at the bottom of the queue

Admin:

The party host can manipulate the queue
